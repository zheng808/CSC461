POTENTIAL FLAWS

Both Microsoft hololens and Apple ARkit have some common technology difficulties waited to be solved:
1) Power conumption: Both simultaneous localization and mapping and computer vision which are all caculation heavy tasks are all power consuming.
2) Tracking limited with not enough light: If there is not enough light in the room, it will be hard to find features and tracking will be limited.
3) Localization and mapping accuracy: This will be a huge problem in the future AR area.

Only for ARkit:
1) Ambient Occlusion: If there is a tree between you and the virtual hologram, the hologram will not be displayed behind the real tree correctly.
2) Lack of cross platform ability: Only support iOS system.
