Augumented Reality

Unlike virtual(VR) reaity, which place a user inside a totall artificial environent, Augumented Reality(AR) is the combination of digital data and real-world human sensory input in real-time that is apparently attached to the physical space. With AR, the digital information appears to become part of the real world, at least in the users's perception.

There are three key components of AR: 1)Combines virtual and real information, with real world as the primary place of action 2)Interactive in real-time 3)Registered in 3D

AR is very powerful for solving everyday problems, having fun, learning with firsthand experience, etc. Today, there are several companies working on the updated AR technology. In June 2017, Apple released ARKit for iOS and in August 2017, Google released ARCore SDK for Android. These two handheld AR technology are widely used in lots of applicationsï¼šPokemon Go, IKEA Place, American Airlines, Sky Guide, Measure, etc. The most popular one is Pokemon Go game that brought AR to the general public in 2016. Another trending AR technology is based on more sophisticated devices: Microsoft HoloLens, Metavision's Meta headsets, etc.
